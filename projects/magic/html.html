MagicPad

2019

  • Responsibilities

    Creator

MagicPad is a free and open-source encryption suite that allow users to easily create encryption key sets and encrypt and decrypt text and other files.

Most, if not all, encryption tools use complicated language that is hard for a regular person to understand. MagicPad uses task-oriented language for a pain-free experience. MagicPad also has an integrated steganography engine for hiding text in images as well as storing encryption keys in images.

MagicPad has over 6,000 downloads totals via NPM, executables, and over 5,000 web-app users.

MagicPost

2019

  • Responsibilities

    Creator

Part of the Magic family, MagicPost is a free and open-source service that allow users to upload, search, and view encrypted messages anonymously. It was designed to serve users with a similar experience as Pastebin.

MagicPost was originally designed as a central point of storage for encrypted messages. The messages are served anonymously and without any tracking.
